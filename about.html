About PageKart

Your friendly digital library for free, curated eBooks across genres.

PageKart is a passion project built to help readers around the world access free, valuable eBooks with ease. Whether you're into self-improvement, productivity or personal-finance - PageKart brings top-quality reads, all in one place.

We believe in the power of knowledge and want to make it accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. More featyres coming soon like user accounts, book uploads, and more categories.
